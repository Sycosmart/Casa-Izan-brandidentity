# Casa Izan — Brand Book

Interactive brand book landing page for **Casa Izan**, a Bolivian wellness lifestyle brand.

**Tagline:** *Vivir con carácter. Vivir en casa.*

## 🎨 About

Casa Izan is a philosophy-turned-brand: Latin American wellness that refuses to choose between ambition and peace of mind. This landing page serves as the interactive brand book — a document clients, designers, and stakeholders can explore to understand the full identity system.

## ✨ Features

- **Preloader** with animated brand mark
- **Scroll progress indicator** and floating section navigator
- **Animated hero** with word-reveal typography and cursor-following blob
- **Interactive color palette** — click to copy HEX codes
- **Typography showcase** with live previews
- **Logo variations gallery** — 6 lockups across brand colors
- **Platform previewer** — see the brand live across 6 touchpoints:
  - Instagram profile & grid
  - Website hero
  - Mobile app UI
  - Business card (front + back, 3D hover)
  - Email signature
  - Physical signage
- **Voice & tone guide** with Do's and Don'ts
- **Interactive brand checklist** with progress tracking
- **Print-friendly** — export to PDF directly from the browser
- **Fully responsive**
